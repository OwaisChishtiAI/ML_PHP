1) Run XAMPP or any other server.
2) Navigate to localhost:port/phpmyadmin (SQL server)
3) Navigate to localhost:port/ML_SQL (To download csv file)
4) Place that csv file to your project directory. XAMPP/htdocs in my case.
5) then, Navigate to localhost:port/ML_SQL/html_tables.php
6) Here, you'll have 3 lists namely,
* Average, minimum and maximum for every 10 values in Array form.
* Average, minimum and maximum for every 10 values in Tabular form.
* Labels for category of Speed factors.
